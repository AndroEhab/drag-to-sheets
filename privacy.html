Privacy Policy for Drag to Sheets

Last updated: July 2025

1. Data Collection and Processing

Drag to Sheets processes spreadsheet files (CSV, TSV, XLSX, XLS) that you provide through drag-and-drop, file picker, or URL import. All file parsing, cleaning, and merging happens locally on your device. Your data is never sent to our servers or any third-party service except as described below.

2. Google Sheets and Google Drive Integration

When you click "Open in Sheets", your file data is uploaded to Google Sheets (and optionally Google Drive for native Excel conversion) using the official Google Sheets and Drive APIs. This extension uses OAuth 2.0 authentication via your Google account to create and populate spreadsheets on your behalf. The OAuth scopes requested are:

  • spreadsheets — to create and edit Google Sheets spreadsheets
  • drive.file — to upload files to your Google Drive (only files created by this extension)

No data is stored on Google Drive or Sheets without your explicit action. Files are only uploaded when you click the upload button.

3. URL Import

The URL import feature fetches spreadsheet files from URLs you provide. The extension makes a direct request from your browser to the specified URL. No intermediary servers are involved. The fetched data is processed locally and only leaves your device when you choose to open it in Google Sheets.

4. Data Storage

To provide session continuity, the extension stores your current file list and preferences using Chrome's built-in storage APIs:

  • chrome.storage.session — holds current files and parsing state (cleared when Chrome restarts)
  • chrome.storage.local — holds your preferences (cleaning options, open mode, mappings)
  • IndexedDB — used as a fallback for large file batches when session storage quota is exceeded

All stored data remains on your device. No analytics, telemetry, or tracking code is included in this extension.

5. Third-Party Services

This extension uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sheets API / Google Drive API — to create and edit spreadsheets in your Google account
  • Google Identity API — for OAuth 2.0 authentication

No other third-party services receive your data.

6. Data Retention

Parsed file data in chrome.storage.session is automatically cleared when the browser restarts. IndexedDB session data can be cleared by clearing the extension's data through Chrome's settings. Google Sheets spreadsheets you create remain in your Google account until you delete them.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@dragtosheets.app.
